TEF Section B — Complete Mastery Guide: Chapter Layout

Analysis of What We're Working With

Your original text contains 3 major documents:

  1. Complete Mastery Guide (7 parts — architecture, phases, expressions, scenarios, practice)
  2. Pre-Manufactured Sentences Bank (271 sentences across 13 categories)
  3. Complete Idiom Bank (200 idioms across 13 categories + bonus combinations)

The Strategy

Instead of keeping these as 3 separate documents, we merge everything by topic. Each objection chapter becomes a complete resource — the guide explanation + all relevant sentences + all relevant idioms + scenario dialogues — all in one place. No jumping between documents.

Following the sample2.txt philosophy: each chapter opens with a real exam situation, walks through the WHY before the HOW, shows the thinking process, builds on previous chapters, and never repeats what was already explained.

What Gets Elaborated (Beyond Original)

For every chapter, following the sample2.txt philosophy:

  • Real exam situation opens each chapter ("You're sitting in the exam room, the examiner says X...")
  • WHY before HOW — why does this objection type exist? What's the examiner testing?
  • Thinking process — "The examiner said Y → I recognize this as a [category] objection → I use RARE → I deploy sentence #X with idiom #Y"
  • Before/After — what a weak response looks like vs. a strong one
  • Common mistakes — what candidates actually do wrong at this stage
  • What to NEVER do — phrases/behaviors that lose points
  • How to verify — how do you know your response landed well?
  • Progressive complexity — each chapter references but never repeats previous chapters
